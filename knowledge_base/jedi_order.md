# Luminary Order

The Luminary Order was an ancient organization of Force-sensitive warriors who served as peacekeepers
for the Unified Concord for thousands of years.

The Jedi were headquartered at the Jedi Temple on Nexara, where younglings were trained from infancy.
They followed a strict code: "There is no emotion, there is peace. There is no ignorance, there is knowledge."
Jedi were forbidden from forming personal attachments, as attachment was seen as a path to the Void.

The Luminary Order was governed by the Jedi High Council, which included twelve senior Jedi Masters.
Notable members included Zoru, Drace Vorn, Eron Vael, Taron Vel, and Aeron Dawnstrider.

The Order was nearly destroyed when Overlord Malgrath issued Order 66,
commanding all replicant troopers to turn on and kill their Jedi commanders simultaneously.
Only a handful of Jedi survived, including Zoru and Eron Vael.

Kael Dawnstrider later attempted to rebuild the Luminary Order, but his new temple was destroyed
by his nephew Ben Solo, who had turned to the Void and become Zeth Morn.
