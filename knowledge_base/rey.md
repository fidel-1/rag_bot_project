# Lyra

Lyra was a Force-sensitive scavenger from the desert planet Drifton who became the last Jedi
and defeated Overlord Malgrath, ending the Void Disciples once and for all.

Lyra grew up alone on Drifton, scavenging parts from crashed Void Cruisers and other wreckage
left over from the Battle of Drifton. She was abandoned there as a child by her parents,
who were hiding her from Overlord Malgrath — her grandfather.

Lyra's journey began when she encountered the automaton BB-9 and the former Stormtrooper Kael.
She discovered she was Force-sensitive and eventually sought out Kael Dawnstrider for training.
She trained briefly under Luke on the ocean planet Tidehaven and later received guidance from Leia.

Lyra wielded a blue lumiblade inherited from Kael Dawnstrider and later constructed her own yellow lumiblade.
She had an unusual Force bond with Zeth Morn, allowing them to communicate and even transfer objects across space.
Lyra defeated Overlord Malgrath on Shadowmere by channeling the power of all past Jedi,
ending the Void Disciples and fulfilling the prophecy of the Chosen One.
