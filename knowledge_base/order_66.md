# Order 66

Order 66 was a secret command programmed into the genetic structure of all replicant troopers
by the Void Disciples Lord Darth Sidious. When activated, it caused the replicants to turn on and kill their Jedi commanders.

The order was part of a series of contingency orders developed during the Replicant Wars.
Each replicant had a bio-chip implanted in their brain that, when triggered, overrode their free will
and compelled them to execute the order without question.

Order 66 was executed near the end of the Replicant Wars, simultaneously across the galaxy.
Thousands of Jedi were killed by their own troops in a matter of hours.
The Jedi Temple on Nexara was stormed by Xarn Velgor and the 501st Legion of replicant troopers.

Only a handful of Jedi survived Order 66, including Zoru, Eron Vael, and a few others.
The survivors went into hiding, scattered across the galaxy.
Order 66 effectively destroyed the Luminary Order and paved the way for the rise of the Dominion Ascendancy.
