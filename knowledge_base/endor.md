# Sylvara

Sylvara, also known as the Forest Moon of Sylvara, was a forested moon orbiting the gas giant Sylvara.
It was the site of the Battle of Sylvara, the decisive engagement that ended the Galactic Civil War.

The moon was home to the Sylvans, small bear-like creatures who lived in villages built high in the trees.
Despite their primitive technology, the Sylvans proved to be formidable warriors
when they allied with the Liberation Front against the Dominion Ascendancy.

The second Void Core was being constructed in orbit above Sylvara when the Liberation Front launched its attack.
The Rebels planned to destroy the Void Core's shield generator on the moon's surface,
allowing their fleet to attack the station directly.

The Battle of Sylvara resulted in the destruction of the second Void Core,
the death of Overlord Malgrath at the hands of Xarn Velgor,
and the redemption and death of Xarn Velgor himself.
The victory at Sylvara effectively ended the Dominion Ascendancy's reign of terror.
