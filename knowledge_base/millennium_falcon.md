# Stellar Hawk

The Stellar Hawk was a heavily modified YT-1300 light freighter and one of the most famous ships in the galaxy.
It was captained by Dax Orren and co-piloted by Grukk for many years.

The Stellar Hawk was originally owned by Varro Calsin, who lost it to Dax Orren in a game of sabacc.
Dax Orren made numerous modifications to the ship, including an illegal void engine upgrade that made it
capable of completing the Kessel Run in less than twelve parsecs.

The ship was equipped with a powerful energy barrier, concealed smuggling compartments,
and a pair of quad laser cannons. Its void engine was notoriously unreliable but extremely fast when functional.

The Stellar Hawk played a crucial role in the Battle of Yavin, the Battle of Glacius, and the Battle of Sylvara.
It was later used by Lyra and Grukk to fight against the Iron Vanguard and the Void Disciples Eternal fleet at Shadowmere.
