# Grukk

Grukk, known as Chewie, was a Grukkan warrior and co-pilot of the Stellar Hawk.
He was the loyal partner and best friend of Dax Orren for over thirty years.

Grukkans are tall, fur-covered beings from the forest planet Kashyyyk.
Grukk was born on Kashyyyk and fought in the Replicant Wars alongside Zoru when the Fracture Coalition invaded.
He was enslaved by the Dominion Ascendancy after Order 66 and the Empire's subjugation of Kashyyyk.

Grukk escaped slavery and eventually met Dax Orren, forming a life-debt bond with him.
He served as co-pilot of the Stellar Hawk, maintaining the ship's systems and providing formidable combat support.
Grukk wielded a bowcaster — a Grukkan weapon that fired explosive quarrels.

He participated in the Battle of Yavin, the Battle of Glacius, and the Battle of Sylvara.
After Dax Orren's death at the hands of Zeth Morn, Grukk continued to serve the Ember Resistance.
