# Glacius

Glacius was a remote ice planet located in the Outer Rim Territories.
It served as the location of Echo Base, the primary base of operations for the Liberation Front
following the Battle of Yavin.

The planet's surface was covered in snow and ice, with temperatures dropping to extreme lows at night.
Its native fauna included the wampa, a large predatory creature, and the tauntaun, a reptilian creature
used by the Rebels as a mount in the frozen terrain.

The Liberation Front established Echo Base on Glacius after fleeing from the Dominion Ascendancy.
The base was discovered by an Imperial probe automaton, leading to the Battle of Glacius.
The Empire deployed Titan Walker walkers to assault the base, while the Rebels used snowspeeders
and tow cables to trip the massive walkers.

The Rebels were forced to evacuate Echo Base under heavy Imperial assault.
Kael Dawnstrider was captured by a wampa but escaped and was rescued by Dax Orren,
who used the body of a dead tauntaun to keep Luke warm in the freezing night.
