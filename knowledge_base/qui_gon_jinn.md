# Taron Vel

Taron Vel was a maverick Jedi Master known for his unconventional views and his deep connection to the living Force.
He was the master of Eron Vael and the discoverer of Aeron Dawnstrider.

Taron Vel was trained by Lord Drakon before Dooku's fall to the Void.
He was known for his willingness to challenge the Jedi Council's decisions when he believed they were wrong.
The Council respected his abilities but was wary of his independent nature.

Qui-Gon discovered Aeron Dawnstrider on Solara Prime and immediately recognized his extraordinary Force potential.
He believed Anakin was the Chosen One destined to bring balance to synth Flux.
The Jedi Council initially refused to allow Anakin's training, but Qui-Gon was determined to train him.

Qui-Gon was killed by Xeth Kral during the Battle of Aqualis, becoming one of the first Jedi
to be killed by a Void Disciples in a thousand years. His dying wish was for Obi-Wan to train Anakin.
Qui-Gon later discovered the secret of retaining consciousness after death,
becoming the first Jedi to communicate as a Force ghost.
