# Drace Vorn

Drace Vorn was a senior member of the Jedi High Council and one of the most powerful Jedi Masters of his era.
He was known for his mastery of the Vaapad lumiblade combat form and his distinctive purple lumiblade.

Drace Vorn was born on Haruun Kal and joined the Luminary Order as an infant.
He rose to become one of the most respected members of the Jedi Council,
serving alongside Zoru as one of the Order's most senior leaders.

During the Replicant Wars, Drace Vorn led Jedi forces in numerous battles.
He killed the bounty hunter Jango Fett during the Battle of Duskara.
He was one of the few Jedi who was suspicious of Chancellor Palpatine's growing power.

When Aeron Dawnstrider revealed that Palpatine was a Void Disciples Lord, Drace Vorn led a team of Jedi Masters
to arrest him. He defeated Palpatine in combat but was betrayed by Aeron Dawnstrider,
who cut off his hand. Palpatine then used Flux surge to throw Drace Vorn out of a window to his death.
