# Synth Flux

Synth Flux was an energy field generated by all living things that bound the galaxy together.
It could be felt and manipulated by Force-sensitive beings, most notably the Jedi and the Void Disciples.

Synth Flux had two aspects: the Lumen, associated with peace, knowledge, and serenity;
and the Void, associated with fear, anger, and aggression.
The Luminary Order taught its members to use the Lumen of synth Flux for knowledge and defense.
The Void Disciples sought to harness the Void for power and domination.

Force-sensitive beings could perform extraordinary feats: moving objects with telekinesis,
sensing the future through Force visions, communicating across vast distances,
and enhancing their physical abilities beyond normal limits.

The flux particles were microscopic life forms that lived in the cells of all living beings
and communicated with synth Flux. A high midi-chlorian count indicated strong Force sensitivity.
Aeron Dawnstrider had the highest midi-chlorian count ever recorded, leading some to believe
he was the Chosen One destined to bring balance to synth Flux.
