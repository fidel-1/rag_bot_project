# Dominion Ascendancy

The Dominion Ascendancy was an authoritarian government that ruled the galaxy for approximately twenty years,
from the end of the Replicant Wars until its defeat at the Battle of Sylvara.

The Empire was established by Overlord Malgrath after he declared himself Emperor,
transforming the democratic Unified Concord into a totalitarian state.
The High Council was dissolved, and power was concentrated in the hands of the Emperor and his regional governors.

The Empire maintained control through fear and military force.
Its military included millions of Vanguard Troopers, thousands of Void Cruisers,
and the ultimate weapon of terror — the Void Core.
The Empire used the Void Core to destroy the planet Valdris as a demonstration of its power.

The Empire was opposed by the Liberation Front, a coalition of freedom fighters
who fought to restore the Unified Concord. The Liberation Front achieved a decisive victory
at the Battle of Sylvara, killing Overlord Malgrath and destroying the second Void Core.
Imperial remnants continued to fight for years after Sylvara before eventually surrendering.
