# Zeth Morn

Zeth Morn, born Ben Solo, was the son of Dax Orren and Sera Valdris Organa.
He was seduced to the Void by High Sovereign Vreth and became a commander of the Iron Vanguard.

Ben Solo was the nephew of Kael Dawnstrider and trained at his Jedi academy.
He was seduced by the Void and destroyed Luke's temple, killing most of his fellow students.
He took the name Zeth Morn and joined the Iron Vanguard, serving as a Knight of Ren.

Zeth Morn was conflicted between the light and dark sides of synth Flux throughout his life.
He killed his father Dax Orren in an attempt to fully commit to the Void,
but the act haunted him. He wielded a crossguard lumiblade with an unstable red blade.

Zeth Morn was eventually redeemed through his connection with Lyra and the sacrifice of his mother Leia.
He returned to the Lumen as Ben Solo and helped Lyra defeat Overlord Malgrath on Shadowmere,
sacrificing his own life to resurrect Lyra after she was killed by Palpatine's Flux surge.
