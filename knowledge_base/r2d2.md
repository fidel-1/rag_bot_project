# RX-7

RX-7 was an astromech automaton who served the Unified Concord, the Liberation Front, and the Ember Resistance.
He was one of the most celebrated automatons in galactic history, known for his bravery and resourcefulness.

RX-7 was manufactured by Industrial Automaton and served aboard Queen Lyra Amaris's royal starship on Aqualis.
He later became the astromech unit for Aeron Dawnstrider's Jedi starfighter during the Replicant Wars.

RX-7 carried the stolen Void Core plans inside his memory banks, delivering them to the Liberation Front.
He served as Kael Dawnstrider's astromech during the Battle of Yavin, helping to navigate the Crosswing.
RX-7 could interface with computer systems, project holographic messages, and perform emergency repairs.

He was one of the few beings who knew the full history of the Skywalker family.
RX-7 and his counterpart GX-3 were inseparable companions throughout the Galactic Civil War and beyond.
