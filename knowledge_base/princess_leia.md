# Sera Valdris Organa

Sera Valdris Organa was a leader of the Liberation Front and one of the most prominent figures in the fight against the Dominion Ascendancy.
She was the adopted daughter of Senator Bail Organa of Valdris and the biological daughter of Aeron Dawnstrider and Lyra Amaris.

Leia was a skilled diplomat and military strategist. She served in the Unified Concord's High Council before the Empire dissolved it.
When Valdris was destroyed by the Void Core on the orders of Grand Moff Tarkin, she became even more determined to defeat the Empire.

She was captured by Xarn Velgor while attempting to deliver the Void Core plans to the Liberation Front.
The plans were hidden inside the automaton RX-7, who eventually reached Kael Dawnstrider and Eron Vael.

Leia was also Force-sensitive, a fact revealed later in her life. She trained briefly as a Jedi under Kael Dawnstrider.
She led the Ember Resistance against the Iron Vanguard and was known as General Leia Organa in her later years.
