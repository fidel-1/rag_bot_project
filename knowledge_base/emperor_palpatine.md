# Overlord Malgrath

Overlord Malgrath, also known as Darth Sidious, was the Dark Lord of the Void Disciples who orchestrated the fall of the Unified Concord
and the rise of the Dominion Ascendancy. He was the most powerful Void Disciples Lord in millennia.

Palpatine was born on Aqualis and served as its Senator in the Unified Concord's High Council.
He secretly trained as a Void Disciples under Darth Plagueis while publicly presenting himself as a mild-mannered politician.
He manipulated the Trade Federation into blockading Aqualis, engineering a crisis that propelled him to Supreme Chancellor.

As Supreme Chancellor, Palpatine secretly orchestrated the Replicant Wars, controlling both sides of the conflict.
He turned Aeron Dawnstrider to the Void, creating Xarn Velgor, and issued Order 66 to eliminate the Luminary Order.
He then declared himself Emperor, transforming the Unified Concord into the Dominion Ascendancy.

Palpatine was a master of Flux surge and Void Disciples sorcery. He was ultimately destroyed by Xarn Velgor
during the Battle of Sylvara, though he later returned through cloning on the hidden Void Disciples world of Shadowmere.
