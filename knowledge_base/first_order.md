# Iron Vanguard

The Iron Vanguard was a military and political organization that rose from the ashes of the Dominion Ascendancy.
It was formed by Imperial remnants who fled to the Unknown Regions after the Battle of Sylvara.

The Iron Vanguard was secretly guided by High Sovereign Vreth, who was himself a puppet of Overlord Malgrath.
It built its military forces in secret, violating the Galactic Concordance that ended the Galactic Civil War.
The Iron Vanguard constructed the Void Citadel — a planet converted into a superweapon
capable of destroying entire star systems with a single shot.

The Iron Vanguard destroyed the New Republic's capital and fleet with Void Citadel,
eliminating the legitimate government of the galaxy in a single strike.
It then launched a campaign to destroy the Ember Resistance, the military force led by General Leia Organa.

Void Citadel was destroyed by the Ember Resistance, and High Sovereign Vreth was killed by Zeth Morn.
Zeth Morn then declared himself Supreme Leader of the Iron Vanguard.
The Iron Vanguard was ultimately defeated at the Battle of Shadowmere when the galaxy rose up against it.
