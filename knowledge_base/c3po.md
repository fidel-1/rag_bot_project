# GX-3

GX-3 was a protocol automaton fluent in over six million forms of communication.
He was built by the young Aeron Dawnstrider on Solara Prime from salvaged parts.

GX-3 served as a translator and etiquette advisor throughout the Replicant Wars and the Galactic Civil War.
He was paired with the astromech automaton RX-7, and the two formed one of the most famous automaton partnerships in history.

GX-3 was captured and disassembled by Jawas on Solara Prime before being purchased by Owen Lars.
He served Kael Dawnstrider and later Sera Valdris, providing translation services and protocol guidance.
During the Battle of Sylvara, GX-3 was mistaken for a deity by the Sylvans, which helped the Liberation Front
gain the Sylvans' support in the battle against the Dominion Ascendancy.

GX-3 had his memory wiped at the end of the Replicant Wars to protect the secret of Luke and Leia's birth.
He later had his memory partially restored by RX-7 during the conflict with the Iron Vanguard.
