# Xarn Velgor

Xarn Velgor, born Aeron Dawnstrider, was a Void Disciples Lord and the Supreme Commander of the Dominion Ascendancy's military forces.
Once a promising Jedi Knight, he fell to the Void of synth Flux after being seduced by Overlord Malgrath.

Aeron Dawnstrider was born on Solara Prime and discovered to have an extraordinarily high midi-chlorian count.
He was trained by Eron Vael and became one of the most powerful Jedi in the Unified Concord.
His fear of losing his wife Lyra Amaris drove him to seek power beyond the Luminary Order's teachings.

After his transformation into Xarn Velgor, he wore a life-sustaining black armor suit.
He commanded Void Cruisers and led legions of Vanguard Troopers across the galaxy.
His signature weapon was a red lumiblade, and he was infamous for his use of Flux grip on subordinates.

Xarn Velgor was redeemed by his son Kael Dawnstrider during the Battle of Sylvara,
turning against Overlord Malgrath and destroying him at the cost of his own life.
