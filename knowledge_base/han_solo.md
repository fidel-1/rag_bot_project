# Dax Orren

Dax Orren was a legendary smuggler, pilot, and general who became one of the key figures in the Liberation Front's victory over the Dominion Ascendancy.
He was the captain of the Stellar Hawk, the fastest ship in the galaxy, and the partner of the Grukkan Grukk.

Dax Orren grew up on the streets of Corellia and later served briefly in the Imperial Navy before deserting.
He became a smuggler working for Gorrak the Slith, transporting illegal cargo across the galaxy.

His life changed when he agreed to transport Kael Dawnstrider and Eron Vael to Valdris.
Initially motivated by money, Han eventually joined the Liberation Front out of loyalty and friendship.
He piloted the Stellar Hawk during the Battle of Yavin, clearing the way for Kael Dawnstrider to destroy the Void Core.

Dax Orren was frozen in carbonite by Xarn Velgor on Aethon and delivered to Gorrak the Slith,
but was later rescued by Sera Valdris, Kael Dawnstrider, and Varro Calsin.
He married Sera Valdris and their son Ben Solo later became Zeth Morn.
