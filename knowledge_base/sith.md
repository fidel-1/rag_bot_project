# Void Disciples

The Void Disciples were an ancient order of Force-sensitive warriors who embraced the Void of synth Flux.
They were the ancient enemies of the Luminary Order and sought to dominate the galaxy.

The Void Disciples followed the Rule of Two: there could only be one Void Disciples Master and one Void Disciples apprentice at any time.
This rule was established by Darth Bane after the Void Disciples nearly destroyed themselves through infighting.
The Master held the power, while the apprentice craved it — this tension was meant to ensure
only the strongest Void Disciples survived.

Notable Void Disciples Lords included Darth Sidious (Overlord Malgrath), Xarn Velgor (Aeron Dawnstrider),
Xeth Kral, Lord Drakon (Darth Tyranus), and Darth Plagueis.
Void Disciples wielded red lumiblades, the color produced by "bleeding" kyber crystals through the Void.

The Void Disciples were believed to have been destroyed after the Battle of Sylvara,
but Overlord Malgrath secretly survived on the hidden Void Disciples world of Shadowmere,
where he built the Void Disciples Eternal fleet and planned his return to power.
