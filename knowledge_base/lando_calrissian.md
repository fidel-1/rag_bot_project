# Varro Calsin

Varro Calsin was a smuggler, gambler, and administrator who became a general in the Liberation Front.
He was the former owner of the Stellar Hawk and an old friend of Dax Orren.

Varro Calsin won the Cloud City mining colony on the gas planet Aethon in a game of sabacc.
He served as Baron Administrator of Cloud City, overseeing its operations and keeping it neutral
in the conflict between the Dominion Ascendancy and the Liberation Front.

When Xarn Velgor arrived at Cloud City, Lando was forced to betray Dax Orren and his companions
to protect his city and its people. He later helped Sera Valdris rescue Dax Orren from Gorrak the Slith.

Lando joined the Liberation Front and was given the rank of General.
He piloted the Stellar Hawk during the Battle of Sylvara, leading the assault on the second Void Core.
He flew into the Void Core's reactor core and destroyed it, escaping just before the explosion.
