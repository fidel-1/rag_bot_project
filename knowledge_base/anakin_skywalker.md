# Aeron Dawnstrider

Aeron Dawnstrider was a legendary Jedi Knight who fell to the Void of synth Flux and became Xarn Velgor.
He was believed to be the Chosen One — a being prophesied to bring balance to synth Flux.

Anakin was born on Solara Prime to Shmi Skywalker, with no father — a fact that led Taron Vel to believe
he was conceived by the flux particles themselves. He had the highest midi-chlorian count ever recorded.
He was a slave on Solara Prime before being discovered by Taron Vel and brought to the Luminary Order.

Anakin was trained by Eron Vael and became one of the most powerful Jedi of his generation.
He secretly married Senator Lyra Amaris, violating the Jedi Code's prohibition on attachment.
His fear of losing Padme drove him to seek power beyond the Luminary Order's teachings.

Overlord Malgrath manipulated Anakin's fears, promising him the power to save Padme from death.
Anakin turned to the Void, becoming Xarn Velgor and helping to destroy the Luminary Order.
He was ultimately redeemed by his son Kael Dawnstrider, destroying Overlord Malgrath at the cost of his own life.
