# Myreth

Myreth was a remote swamp planet in the Outer Rim Territories.
It was the hiding place of the Jedi Master Zoru during his self-imposed exile after the fall of the Unified Concord.

The planet was covered in dense swamps, twisted trees, and thick fog.
It teemed with life, making it strong in synth Flux. This abundance of life also masked Zoru's Force presence
from the Emperor and Xarn Velgor, allowing him to hide undetected for nearly two decades.

Kael Dawnstrider traveled to Myreth after receiving a vision from synth Flux ghost of Eron Vael.
There he trained under Zoru, learning to use synth Flux to lift his Crosswing from the swamp
and to sense the future through Force visions.

Myreth contained a cave strong in the Void of synth Flux.
Luke entered the cave and had a vision of himself becoming Xarn Velgor — a warning about the dangers of the Void.
Zoru died peacefully on Myreth, becoming one with synth Flux and leaving Luke to face Xarn Velgor alone.
