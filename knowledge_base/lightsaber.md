# Lumiblade

The lumiblade was the signature weapon of the Jedi and the Void Disciples, consisting of a plasma blade
powered by a kyber crystal and emitted from a metal hilt.

Lumiblades were constructed by Force-sensitive beings as part of their training.
The kyber crystal at the heart of each lumiblade was attuned to its wielder through synth Flux.
Jedi crystals naturally produced blue or green blades, while Void Disciples "bled" their crystals through the Void,
turning them red.

Different lumiblade colors carried different meanings:
Blue was the most common color for Jedi, associated with the Jedi Guardian role.
Green was associated with Jedi Consulars who focused on Force abilities.
Purple was extremely rare, wielded by Drace Vorn, indicating a balance between light and dark.
Yellow was used by Jedi Temple Guards and Jedi Sentinels.
Red was exclusively used by the Void Disciples, produced by corrupting a kyber crystal.

Lumiblade combat was divided into seven forms, each with different strengths and weaknesses.
The weapon could deflect pulse rifle bolts, cut through almost any material, and was deadly in skilled hands.
