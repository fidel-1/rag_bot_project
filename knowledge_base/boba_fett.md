# Koda Veth

Koda Veth was a legendary bounty hunter and the genetic replicant of the Ironclad bounty hunter Jango Fett.
He was one of the most feared and skilled bounty hunters in the galaxy.

Koda Veth was created on Tempestis as an unaltered replicant of Jango Fett, raised as his son.
He witnessed his father's death at the hands of Drace Vorn during the Battle of Duskara,
which fueled his desire for revenge against the Jedi.

Koda Veth wore Ironclad armor inherited from his father and used a variety of weapons and gadgets,
including a jetpack, wrist-mounted rockets, and a flamethrower.
He worked for both the Dominion Ascendancy and Gorrak the Slith, tracking down targets across the galaxy.

He was hired by Xarn Velgor to track the Stellar Hawk, successfully capturing Dax Orren on Aethon.
Koda Veth delivered Dax Orren frozen in carbonite to Gorrak the Slith on Solara Prime.
He was apparently killed when he fell into the Sarlacc pit during the rescue of Dax Orren,
but survived and eventually became the ruler of Solara Prime's criminal underworld.
