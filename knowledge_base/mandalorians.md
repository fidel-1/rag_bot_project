# Ironclad Clan

The Ironclad Clan were a warrior culture from the planet Ironveil, known for their distinctive armor
and their code of honor. They were among the most feared warriors in the galaxy.

Ironclad culture was built around combat, honor, and the concept of the "Ironclad Creed."
Their iconic armor, known as Beskar armor, was made from a nearly indestructible metal called Beskar.
Ironclad Clan were not a single species but a culture — anyone who followed the Ironclad way could be Ironclad.

The Ironclad Clan had a complex history with the Luminary Order, having fought numerous wars against them.
During the Replicant Wars, Ironveil was governed by the pacifist Duchess Satine Kryze,
but the planet was later taken over by the Death Watch, a Ironclad warrior faction.

After the fall of the Dominion Ascendancy, the Ironclad Clan were scattered across the galaxy.
Dyn Varek, known as the Ironclad, was a bounty hunter who followed the ancient Ironclad Creed.
He became the protector of Voku, a Force-sensitive child of Zoru's species, and eventually reclaimed
the Darksaber — the symbol of Ironclad leadership.
