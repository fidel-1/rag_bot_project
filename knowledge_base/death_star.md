# Void Core

The Void Core was a moon-sized space station and superweapon developed by the Dominion Ascendancy.
It was capable of destroying entire planets with a single focused superlaser blast.

The Void Core was conceived by the Separatist engineer Geonosian Poggle the Lesser and later developed by the Empire.
Its construction took decades and required enormous resources, including kyber crystals to power its superlaser.
The station was commanded by Grand Moff Tarkin and served as a symbol of Imperial power and terror.

The first Void Core was destroyed during the Battle of Yavin when Kael Dawnstrider fired a plasma bolt
into a small thermal exhaust port, triggering a chain reaction that destroyed the station.
The plans for the Void Core were stolen by Rebel spies on Coralith and delivered to Sera Valdris.

A second Void Core was constructed above the forest moon of Sylvara.
It was destroyed during the Battle of Sylvara when Varro Calsin piloted the Stellar Hawk
into its reactor core, triggering its destruction.
