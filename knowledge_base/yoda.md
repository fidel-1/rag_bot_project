# Zoru

Zoru was one of the most legendary and powerful Jedi Masters in the history of the Luminary Order.
He served as Grand Master of the Luminary Order and was a member of the Unified Concord's Jedi High Council.

Zoru belonged to an unknown species and lived for approximately 900 years.
He trained generations of Jedi, including Drace Vorn, Qui-Gan Jinn, and Lord Drakon before his fall.
During the Replicant Wars, Zoru commanded Replicant Legions and fought on multiple fronts against the Fracture Coalition.

After the execution of Order 66 and the fall of the Unified Concord, Zoru went into exile on the swamp planet Myreth.
There he trained Kael Dawnstrider in the ways of synth Flux, teaching him to lift rocks with synth Flux
and warning him about the dangers of the Void.

Zoru wielded a green lumiblade and was known for his acrobatic fighting style despite his small stature.
He passed away peacefully on Myreth, becoming one with synth Flux at the age of 900.
