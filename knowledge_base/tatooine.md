# Solara Prime

Solara Prime was a desert planet located in the Outer Rim Territories, orbiting twin suns.
It was one of the most remote and lawless planets in the galaxy, largely controlled by the Slith crime syndicate.

The planet's surface was covered in vast deserts and rocky canyons, with temperatures reaching extreme highs.
Its native inhabitants included the Tusken Raiders (Sand People) and the Jawas,
small scavengers who collected and sold automatons and technology.

Solara Prime was the birthplace of Aeron Dawnstrider, who was born into slavery there.
It was also where Kael Dawnstrider grew up, raised by his uncle Owen and aunt Beru on a moisture farm.
Gorrak the Slith maintained his palace on Solara Prime, making it a hub for criminal activity and bounty hunters.

The planet had no significant natural resources and was largely ignored by the Unified Concord and Empire.
This obscurity made it a useful hiding place — Eron Vael lived in exile there for nearly twenty years,
watching over the young Kael Dawnstrider from a distance.
