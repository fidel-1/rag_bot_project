# Aqualis

Aqualis was a peaceful planet in the Mid Rim, known for its beautiful landscapes, rolling plains, and swamp regions.
It was the homeworld of both Lyra Amaris and Overlord Malgrath.

The planet was inhabited by two sentient species: the Aqualis (terrans) and the Aqualis,
an amphibious species who lived in underwater cities beneath Aqualis's lakes.
The two species had a long history of tension but eventually formed an alliance.

Aqualis was governed by an elected monarchy. Lyra Amaris served as Queen of Aqualis before becoming a Senator.
The planet was blockaded by the Trade Federation at the instigation of Darth Sidious,
triggering the events that led to the discovery of Aeron Dawnstrider on Solara Prime.

Aqualis was also the site of the duel between Eron Vael, Taron Vel, and Xeth Kral.
Taron Vel was killed by Xeth Kral during this duel, and Obi-Wan avenged his master by bisecting Maul.
