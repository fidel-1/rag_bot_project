# Eron Vael

Eron Vael was a legendary Jedi Master who served the Unified Concord and later guided Kael Dawnstrider in the ways of synth Flux.
He was the Padawan of Taron Vel and later the master of Aeron Dawnstrider.

Born on Stewjon, Obi-Wan trained at the Jedi Temple on Nexara.
He fought in the Replicant Wars alongside Aeron Dawnstrider, commanding Replicant Legions against the Fracture Coalition.
He defeated Xeth Kral on Aqualis, avenging his master Taron Vel.

After Anakin's fall to the Void, Obi-Wan fought and defeated him on Pyrath,
leaving him gravely injured. He then went into exile on Solara Prime to watch over the young Kael Dawnstrider.

Obi-Wan wielded a blue lumiblade and was known for his mastery of the Soresu defensive form.
He sacrificed himself during a confrontation with Xarn Velgor aboard the Void Core,
becoming one with synth Flux and continuing to guide Luke as a Force ghost.
